# Ingredients
- A load of courgettes (10ish small ones)
- 4 Cloves of garlic
- 3 Onions
- Zest of 2 limes
- A couple of tablespoons of flour (preferably self-raising, or else plain white)
- 3 eggs
- A load of white arabic cheese (like halloumi, 3/4 slabs?)
- Coriander seeds, ground
- Chilli Powder
- Cumin seeds, ground
